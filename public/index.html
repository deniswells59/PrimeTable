ABOUT US
WHO WE ARE

Prime table is a premier boutique steakhouse in Northern California that specializes in some of the best quality beef available in the U.S.

Much like our sister restaurant Market Tavern, we pride ourselves on making everything from scratch. You can be certain that from the bread, to our stocks, to our desserts, everything is made with pride and love.

While the decor at Prime Table lends itself to a swanky throw-back era, we encourage our guests to come in any time with or without a reservation.

Our bar crafts some of the best old school and new school drinks and we offer a wine list with both local favorites and some high end, rare wine to compliment our beef.

Whether you want a night out enjoying a 28 day dry aged bone in New York, a beautifully, slow cooked, prime rib with mascarpone mashed potatoes and roasted king trumpet mushrooms, or just some snacks and wine at the bar, Prime Table is the place to be.
